The best book to read during your break time.

Book reviwed: Atomic Habits by James Clear

Genuinely helpful in the search for meaning.

I bought the book because it is a bestseller and had great reviews. This book is filled with practical steps and examples. Plenty of people are seeking the right book that resonates with them. The key points in this book are:
  • Compound Effect - Very small changes over time will have a big impact.
  • Habit Building Techniques - Make good habits into routines; use positive reinforcements and other techniques outlined in the book.
  • Monitor and Measure - Keep track of your progress and improvements.

George Murphy

Book reviwed: Ikigai by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles

I wonder what the hype is all about

Except for a few pieces of wisdom, most of the book feels preachy, very much like a sales pitch and boring too. There's no flow or continuity which makes one feel as if they're reading a college student's thesis report. This book is not life changing nor anything. I have been slightly harsh in my criticism, I realise, I hence must reiterate that there definitely is the occasional pearl of wisdom to be found in "ikigai".
